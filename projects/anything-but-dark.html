Overview

Anything but Dark is a 2D platformer focusing mainly on challenge and spacial/environmental puzzle solving, but also on narrative.

The player is able to change the predominantcolours of the environment, which alters the elements present in it as well, having to alternate between red, greenand blue to advance in the level.

My contribution to this project was focused on gameplay programming, as well as some of the systems programming.

If you want to try the game, head over to the project's itch.io page
