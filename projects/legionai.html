Overview

LegionAI is an upgrade of the SimpAI agent, which ran in the ColorShapeLinks AI Competition in 2021.

It is a pure MCTS search algorithm based agent, which made use of genetic algorithms to improve upon itself throughout many generations of playouts.

My contribution to this project consisted mainly of researching and developing the MCTS search algorithm
