Overview

The Seed is a 2D platformer inspired by the movie Inception, with a design mainly focusing on the gravity altering effects of the dreams.

The player is able to alter the effect gravity has on them and to shape-shift into an enemy while near one of them, having to use these abilities effectively to escape a labyrinthic building.

My contribution to this project was mainly focused on game/level design and programming, having produced the enemy sprite while learning game art in another curricular unit.

If you want to try the game, head over to the project's itch.io page
