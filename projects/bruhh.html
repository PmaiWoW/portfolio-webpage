Overview

Bruhh is a local multiplayer 3D brawler, with ragdoll physics incorporated, focusing mainly on dumb fun.

The players choose which characters they want to play as and which arena they will fight in, having the option to AI agents as well. Once in the arena, the players can make use of different attacks like punching, kicking, grabbing and throwing enemies, as well as picking up weapons to use against them.

My contribution to this project was focused on systems programming, as well as some of the gameplay programming.

If you want to try the game, head over to the project's itch.io page
