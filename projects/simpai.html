Overview

SimpAI is an AI agent developed in C# to compete in the ColorShapeLinks AI Competition in 2020.

It is is a Negamax search algorithm based agent, making use of many optimizations such as:

  • Alpha-Beta Pruning
  • Move Ordering
  • Hashtables with Zobrist Hashing/Keys
  • Iterative Deepening
  • Parallelization (Multithreading)
  • Genetic Algorithm (Machine Learning)

The agent also made use of multiple heuristics with different weights adjusted by the genetic algorithm, which guides the search algorithm by evaluating boards as it thinks.

My contribution to this project consisted mainly of researching, developing the search algorithm and writting the paper that the team published, as well as assisting in some parts of the heuristics implementation and in the genetic algorithm.
